About My Final

Welcome to Night Mode

For my final, I am going to create a website from the point of view of someone trying to prove that her brother was murdered. I’ve come up with a whole case to base this project on. A man named Peter Bonsby was found dead in his apartment, stabbed in the chest. The character who is creating the website, Anna Bonsby, is Peter’s sister. His death was ruled as a suicide, but Anna doesn’t believe that. She thinks that Peter’s girlfriend, Mary, murdered him. The fictitious truth in this case would be Mary murdered Peter.
